Title of the app

This is GSearch, a clone, somewhat, of the GitHub search. You can search for users (the current implemantation of the project) and details about them will be displayed for you. You are able to view their Login names, how popular they are (Followers Vs Following) and lastly how many Repositories they have (publicly). Finally, you can also check them out on their actual GitHub page!
